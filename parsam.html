About ParSam

Founded in 2026, ParSam Learning Centre is a private secondary school located at 117 First Avenue, Prospect Waterfalls, Harare. We are dedicated to academic excellence and holistic development, offering a safe, technologically integrated environment.

Our Vision

To Inspire, Educate And Inspire

Academic Programmes

Form 1 to Form 6 – ZIMSEC Curriculum. We offer Sciences, Commercials, Arts and A Strong Emphasis On Digital Literacy.

O-Level Subjects

  • ✓ Mathematics, Combined Science & Pure Sciences
  • ✓ English Language, Shona Language & Literature
  • ✓ Geography, Computer Science & Statistics
  • ✓ History, Heritage, and FRS
  • ✓ Principle Of Accounting, Economics and Commerce

A-Level Subjects

  • ✓ Pure Mathematics, Biology, Chemistry and Physics
  • ✓ English Literature, Shona Literature and Heritage
  • ✓ Geography, Business Studies (BS) and Computer Science
  • ✓ Economic History, History, Heritage and FRS
  • ✓ Accounting, Economics and Statistics

Extra-Curricular

Athletics, Soccer, Volleyball, Music and Art, Netball, Basketball, Tennis, Swimming, Darts, Pool, Chess, Robotics, Public Speaking. Regular inter-school competitions.
